📡 About Romelyn & Connect

⚡ Future Electronics Engineer

I'm Romelyn Tagurigan, a 4th-year Electronics Engineering student at Iloilo Science and Technology University (ISAT U). My fascination with electronics started when I first held a soldering iron — since then, I've dedicated myself to learning circuit design, embedded programming, and hardware debugging.

I have experience leading university workshops about PCB manufacturing and actively contribute to open-source hardware communities. My goal is to design sustainable and innovative electronic systems that solve real-world problems, from smart agriculture to assistive technology.
